# NABDA AI

AI-powered Infrastructure Protection Platform

---

## Overview

NABDA is an AI-driven system built to predict and prevent infrastructure risk across:

- Electricity load and tariff tiers
- Water consumption anomalies
- Air quality intelligence
- Carbon footprint modeling

NABDA transforms infrastructure from reactive to predictive.

---

## Core Features

- Electricity Tier Jump Prediction
- Water Leak Risk Scoring
- Weekly AI Protection Reports
- Carbon Intelligence Layer
- Portfolio Risk Modeling

---

## System Architecture

Frontend  
Real-time dashboard for monitoring, alerts, and risk visualization

Backend  
Node.js application integrated with Gemini API

AI Layer  
Predictive modeling, anomaly detection, financial impact estimation, automated reporting

---

## Weekly AI Protection Protocol

- Sunday — Electricity review
- Tuesday — Water anomaly detection
- Daily — Air quality monitoring
- Weekly — Full protection and savings report

---

## Business Model

- B2B SaaS annual subscription
- Scaled pricing by facility or portfolio size
- Optional performance-based savings model
- Enterprise carbon reporting services
